# UZH-FPV Quadcopter Dataset: Indoor Forward-Facing (DAVIS-3 APS) Baseline
A monocamera visual-inertial drone racing dataset derived from the University of Zurich (UZH) FPV tracking repository. Consists of a continuous indoor aggressive flight sequence using forward-looking, high-rate hardware-synchronized sensor arrays.

Key Specifications:
- Perception Mode: Monochromatic APS (Active Pixel Sensor) global shutter frames (640x480 resolution) extracted from a neuromorphic DAVIS240C camera sensor.
- Estimation Reference: High-rate (approx. 500 Hz) IMU linear accelerations and angular velocities synced with millimeter-accurate Leica laser optical ground truth metrics.
- Pipeline Objective: Optimized baseline for training YOLO-Pose 4-point geometric corner tracking and testing multithreaded non-ROS C++ state estimation filters (EKF).
